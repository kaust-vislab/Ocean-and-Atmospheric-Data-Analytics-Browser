Oceanic and Atmospheric Data Analytics Browser

Created by Shehzad Afzal, Mazen Hittawe, Sohaib Ghani, Tahira Jamil, Omar Knio, Marcus Hadwiger, and Ibrahim Hoteit

King Abdullah University of Science & Technology, KSA

This browser is created to display state-of-the-art work done in visual analysis approaches for ocean and atmospheric datasets. We surveyed hundreds of papers from several journals and conferences and shortlisted these most relevant papers.

The design of this browser is inspired by the Text Visualization Browser created by the ISOVIS Group.
